237 at First

Project Details

CLIENT:

Chris Shaffer, Real Estate Broker at Newmark Cornish & Carey

CREATIVE FIELDS:

Presentation Design

SUMMARY:

237 at First is a project developed by South Bay Development. The objective of the presentation was to compile market data in the office and R&D markets in San Francisco South Bay area to present to South Bay Development to show the current market trends. This project was done in collaboration with the market research team.

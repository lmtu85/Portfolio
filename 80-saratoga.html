Project Details

CLIENT:

Jesse Millman, Real Estate Broker at Newmark Knight Frank

CREATIVE FIELDS:

Presentation Design

SUMMARY:

This presentation package is called a Broker Opinion of Value. The content covers a detailed description and value of the subject property, along with graphical and statistical data of the surrounding area, to present to the broker’s client. It acts as a reference guide for the client to make a sale investment. I worked in collaboration with the market research team for the statistical market data content.

Senior Night

Project Details

CLIENT:

Todd Shaffer, Real Estate Broker

CREATIVE FIELDS:

Print Design

SUMMARY:

Mountain View High, home of the spartans, does a senior night for their graduating seniors in the girls volleyball team each year. Todd Shaffer was the designated parent representative to create a gift for the seniors. He came to me with two ideas, one was a 24”x36” jersey poster with the girl’s last name and number and also a 11”x17” photo collage for each. He provided all the needed content and gave me the freedom to come up with the designs. There were 7 seniors that year.
